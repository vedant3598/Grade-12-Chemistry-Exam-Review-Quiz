We have 8 planets in our solar system and their names are:

Mercury
  1. Smallest planet in the Solar System
  2. Closet planet from the Sun
  3. Diameter: 4,880 km
  4. Mass: 3.30 x 10^23kg
  5. It takes Mercury about 88 Earth days to orbit around the Sun.
  6. Although Mercury does not have a lot of atmosphere, it has small amounts of oxygen, helium, and oxygen. It has even small parts of potassium, sodium, calcium, and magnesium.
Venus
  1. Third biggest planet in the Solar System
  2. Second closest planet from the Sun
  3. Temperatures on Venus can reach upto 465 degrees Celsius.
  4. Diameter: 4,880 km
  5. Mass: 3.30 x 10^24 kg
  6. It takes Venus about 225 Earth days to orbit around the Sun.
  7. It is one of the inner planets and is known as a terrestial planet.
  8. Venus consists mainly of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, and small amounts of sulfur dioxide, argon, water, carbon monoxide, helium and neon.
Earth
  1. Fourth biggest planet in the Solar System
  2. Third closest planet from the Sun
  3. Diameter: 12,756.3 km
  4. Mass: 5.972 x 10^23 kg
  5. It takes Earth about 365 days to orbit around the Sun.
  6. Earth consists mainly of hyrdogen, nitrogen, oxygen, argon, water, and carbon dioxide.
Mars
  1. Seventh biggest planet in the Solar System
  2. Fourth closet planet from the Sun.
  3. Diameter: 6,794 km
  4. Mass: 6.39 x 10^23 kg
  5. It takes Mars about 687 Earth days to orbit around the Sun.
  6. Mars atmosphere consists of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, argon, with small amounts of oxygen and water.
Jupiter
  1. Biggest planet in the Solar System
  2. Fifth closet planet from the Sun.
  3. Diameter: 139,822 km
  4. Mass: 1.898 x 10^27 kg
  5. It takes Jupiter about 12 Earth years to orbit around the Sun.
  6. Jupiter atmosphere consists of hydrogen, helium, and small amounts of methane, ammonia, hydrogen sulfide and water.
Saturn
  1. Second biggest planet in the Solar System
  2. Sixth closet planet from the Sun.
  3. Diameter: 116,464 km
  4. Mass: 5.683 x 10^26 kg
  5. It takes Saturn about 29 Earth years to orbit around the Sun.
  6. Saturn's atmosphere consists of hydrogen, helium, and small amounts of methane and water ice.
Uranus
  1. Sixth biggest planet in the Solar System
  2. Seventh closet planet from the Sun.
  3. Diameter: 50,724 km
  4. Mass: 8.681 x 10^25 kg
  5. It takes Uranus about 84 Earth years to orbit around the Sun.
  6. Uranus's atmosphere consists of hydrogen, helium, and small amounts of methane, ammonia, and water.
Neptune
  1. Fifth biggest planet in the Solar System
  2. Eigth closet planet from the Sun.
  3. Diameter: 49,244 km
  4. Mass: 1.024 x 10^26 kg
  5. It takes Neptune about 165 Earth years to orbit around the Sun.
  6. Jupiter's atmosphere consists of hydrogen, helium, and small amounts of water, ammonia and other ices.
